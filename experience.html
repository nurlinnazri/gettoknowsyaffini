Throughout my studies at UiTM, I had the experience of participating in a self discipline camp to complete my co-curricular subject in the field of Taekwondo Tempur Tanpa Senjata (TTS). We successfully conducted this character-building camp 2km from the UiTM area. I was able to hone my teamwork skills, communication skills, survival skills, and self-defense skills from this camp.
